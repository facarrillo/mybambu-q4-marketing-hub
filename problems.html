üìß Campaign F: International Money Transfer Holiday Push

Campaign Overview

Objective: Increase IMT transactions 20% during Nov-Dec
Audiences: All active users, Previous IMT users, Lost customers with IMT history + 9 country-specific segments
Target: 20% increase in IMT transactions
Channels: Email (bilingual), In-App Messages, Push Notifications

Strategy

Aggressive multi-touch holiday campaign focused on IMT transactions (revenue doesn't grow organically during holidays like Card does). Five-email sequence timed to key holiday moments: Holiday announcement, Thanksgiving family gratitude, end-of-month bill support, Christmas prep, and last-minute urgency. Emphasize $0.85 pricing advantage and emotional connection to family.

NEW: Country-Specific Personalization

Segment users by their destination country (Top 9: Mexico, Honduras, Nicaragua, Colombia, Guatemala, Ecuador, El Salvador, Dominican Republic, Venezuela) and personalize email copy to mention their specific country and trusted local payers/banks. This increases relevance and trust.

Example Personalization:

  • Mexico users: "Send money to Mexico for just $0.85 - receive at Elektra Mexico or Bancoppel"
  • Honduras users: "Send money to Honduras for just $0.85 - receive at Banco Atlantida or Elektra Honduras"
  • Generic (non-IMT users): "Send money home for just $0.85"

Key Messages:

  • Holiday season: Send money to [COUNTRY] for just $0.85
  • Family connection: Be there for your family in [COUNTRY] no matter the distance
  • Trusted delivery: Receive at [LOCAL PAYERS] - same day
  • End of month: Help family pay bills on time
  • Christmas prep: Help family buy gifts, cook meals, pay year-end bills
  • Last chance urgency: Christmas is 5 days away

Critical IMT revenue doesn't grow organically during holidays - requires aggressive proactive push!

Personalization Impact Country-specific messaging increases open rates 15-25% and conversion rates 20-30% vs generic messaging.
